About Me

My name is Tao Zhou. I am currently pursuing my Doctor’s degree in College of Information Science & Electronic Engineering with Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the (co-)supervision of Qi Ye, Wenhan Luo, and Zhiguo Shi. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University in 2019.

Research Interest

My research experience lies in 2D perceptual tasks (including detection, multi-object tracking, and video object segmentation). I plan to incorporate foundation models and generative models in my future research.
