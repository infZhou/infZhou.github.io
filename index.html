About Me

Tao Zhou is currently pursuing his Doctor’s degree in College of Information Science & Electronic Engineering with Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the (co-)supervision of Qi Ye, Wenhan Luo, and Zhiguo Shi. He obtained his Bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University in 2019.

Research Interest
